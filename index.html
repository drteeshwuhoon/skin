Profile

Dr Tee Shwu Hoon is a dermatologist and internal medicine physician recognised by the National Specialist Registry, Ministry of Health, Malaysia.

She obtained her undergraduate medical degree from the International Medical University in 2005. She completed her housemanship training in Hospital Tengku Ampuan Afzan, Pahang and then served the general public in a district hospital at Segamat, Johor. Following that, she underwent training in internal medicine and attained the Membership of the Royal College of Physician (United Kingdom) in 2010. While serving as a physician in Hospital Selayang, she was awarded with Excellence Service Award under Ministry of Health in year 2012. Subsequently, she progressed to subspecialty training in the Department of Dermatology, Hospital Kuala Lumpur and Hospital Tengku Ampuan Rahimah. She was awarded Advanced Master in Dermatology from the Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia upon completion of her training.

She is currently the Head of Unit of Dermatology at the Serdang Hospital and a visiting consultant dermatologist at the Columbia Asia Hospital, Puchong in Selangor. Other than her clinical service, she is also interested in medical research and has authored several scientific publications.

She has special interest in eczema, psoriasis, acne, fungal and other skin infections, pigmentary disorders and procedural dermatology.
